## Key tension points (unresolved)

- **Was the collection "stolen"?** The Mansell/Schneider side says yes; BAM says it found only a small
  remnant (~$2,000–$5,000 worth) at the store, offered to return it, and denies theft. **Unadjudicated.**
- **Was the consignment "authorized"?** BAM says consignments were not permitted under franchise rules;
  former franchisee Gorman publicly posted contract language she says permitted consignment. **Disputed.**
- **Did police act improperly?** Schneider alleges bias and excessive force; AFPD's body-cam release
  disputes the injury claim and says it enforced Utah law within its jurisdiction. **Unadjudicated.**
